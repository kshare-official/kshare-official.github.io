## What is Flash Effect AI Prompt

The Flash Effect AI Prompt is designed to replicate how a real camera flash interacts with a subject and environment.

Instead of flat lighting, it introduces:
- Strong directional flash
- High contrast between light and shadow
- Natural skin tones and texture
- Depth through realistic light falloff

This makes AI-generated visuals appear closer to **real-world photography**.
